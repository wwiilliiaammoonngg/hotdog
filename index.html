Super Special Hot Dogs

The story behind the Super Special Hot Dogs goes like this.

I was walking down the street and a hot dog guy was like, "Hey, do you want a super special hot dog?

I was like, "I don't know, whats the deal with the hot dog?"

He said, "Oh, you haven't heard of my hot dogs?"

I said, "No."

And he said, "Do you wanna try?"

And I said, "What?"

And he said, "Do you wanna try?"

And I said, "Yeah."

And then he said some weird stuff and I can't remember what it was.

So I tried the hot dog, and it was good. So I made my own recipe for the super special hot dog.

The Super Special Hot Dog Recipe

  • Hot Dog Buns | 130-270 cal.

    • 1 bun per hot dog.

  • Hot Dog Weenies | 151-302 cal.

    • 1 - 2 weiners per hot dog.

  • Mustard | 3 cal.

    • Put a bunch of mustard all over the hot dog.

  • Tomatoes | 17 cal.

    • Diced and put underneath the weiner(s).

  • Bat Meat | 80 cal.

    • 12 pieces per Hot Dog

    I know this sounds weird, but you need to trust me.

  • Live Trout | 255 cal.

    • This living trout will provide lots of flavor and a lush sense of life to your super special hot dog.

  • Horseradish | 6 cal.

    • The gnarliest stuff in the world. I like to put a lot of it on there, but you don't have to if it's not your thing.

12 Steps to Make Yourself a Super Special Hot Dog!

  1. Grab a hot dog bun.
  2. Put the hot dog bun on the plate.
  3. Take your hand off of the hot dog bun.
  4. Grab 1 or 2 weiners, it depends on how many you want.
  5. Put the weiner(s) in the hot dog bun.
  6. Let go of the weiner(s)
  7. Dice tomatoes and place them under the weiner(s)
  8. Grab the live trout, and place it on top. Try to keep it from jumping out of the hot dog.
  9. Spread mustard on top of the fish.
  10. Place 12 pieces of cooked bat on top of the trout.
  11. Spread horseradish on the top of it all.
  12. Enjoy!
